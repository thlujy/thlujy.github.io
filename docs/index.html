Bio

Jianyuan is now a Research staff at Alibaba Group. He received his Ph.D. degree in Computer Science from Tsinghua University, Beijing, China in July 2017. His advisor is Prof. Bin Liu. He visited Georgia Institute of Technology from October 2015 to April 2016, supervised by Prof. Jun(Jim) Xu. Previously he received the B.S. degree from Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications, Beijing, China in June 2011.
